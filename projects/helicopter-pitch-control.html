Helicopter Pitch Control: PID Tuning & Response Comparison

Objective: Design, simulate, and analyze a closed-loop PID control system for helicopter pitch stabilization using Simulink.

System Overview

  • Plant model: Second-order pitch dynamics (\( \theta'' = M/I \))
  • Controller: PID (P, I, D)
  • Inputs: Step input (0→10°), Sine disturbance
  • Output: Pitch angle (in degrees)

PID Configurations Tested

Test SetPIDNotes
A20.20.4Stable; settled within ±1° after ~25s
B30.80.5Slight overshoot; faster response
C100Steady-state error (~20°); no I-gain

Why These Outcomes Were Expected

P controls response strength, I eliminates steady-state error, and D dampens oscillations. The plant input is torque, not force—since the wind creates a moment on the rotor.
